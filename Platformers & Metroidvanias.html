FIND YOUR GAMES

Platformers & Metroidvanias

Master movement and exploration in beautifully crafted worlds

Jump, climb, and explore through intricately designed environments. Platformers test your reflexes and timing, while Metroidvanias reward exploration with new abilities that unlock previously inaccessible areas. These games combine precision movement with the joy of discovery.

Super Mario Odyssey

Travel across vast kingdoms with Mario and his new ally Cappy. Capture enemies to use their unique abilities in this joyful 3D platforming adventure that celebrates the history of the Mario franchise.

Hollow Knight

Explore the vast, interconnected world of Hallownest in this beautiful hand-drawn Metroidvania. Encounter bizarre bugs and beasts, discover ancient secrets, and unravel mysteries in a kingdom of insects and heroes.

Metroid Dread

The return of 2D Metroid introduces a new planet to explore and terrifying E.M.M.I. robots to escape from. Samus Aran's latest adventure combines exploration, combat, and stealth in this tense sci-fi action game.

Cuphead: Don't Deal with the Devil

Inspired by 1930s cartoons, this run-and-gun platformer features meticulously hand-drawn animation and a jazz soundtrack. Help Cuphead and Mugman repay their debt to the devil in this challenging game.

Celeste

Help Madeline climb Celeste Mountain in this precision platformer with a moving story about overcoming personal demons. With tight controls and a forgiving checkpoint system, Celeste is both challenging and accessible.

Ori and the Blind Forest

A visually stunning Metroidvania with fluid movement mechanics. Guide Ori through the dying forest of Nibel in this emotional tale of hope and sacrifice, featuring beautiful hand-painted art and orchestral score.

Ori and the Will of the Wisps

The sequel to Blind Forest expands on the original with new combat options and movement abilities. Explore the new land of Niwen in this breathtaking adventure with improved gameplay and emotional storytelling.

Donkey Kong Country: Tropical Freeze

Join Donkey Kong and his friends as they reclaim their island from invading arctic forces. This challenging 2D platformer features beautiful levels, memorable boss fights, and an excellent David Wise soundtrack.

Super Mario 3D World + Bowser's Fury

Experience two Mario adventures in one package. The main game offers classic 3D platforming with friends, while Bowser's Fury introduces an open-world Mario adventure with a rampaging, kaiju-sized Bowser.

Shovel Knight

A love letter to 8-bit platformers with modern design sensibilities. Wield your trusty Shovel Blade through this retro-inspired adventure featuring tight controls, memorable characters, and chiptune music.

Shovel Knight: Specter of Torment

Play as the scythe-wielding Specter Knight in this prequel to Shovel Knight. With unique movement abilities centered around wall running and dashing, this campaign offers a fresh take on the Shovel Knight formula.

Kirby and the Forgotten Land

Kirby's first fully 3D adventure takes place in a mysterious world with abandoned civilizations. With the new "Mouthful Mode" ability, Kirby can inhale large objects to take on their properties in creative ways.

Animal Well

A mysterious puzzle-platformer filled with secrets. Explore an interconnected labyrinth filled with strange creatures and hidden mechanics in this meticulously designed pixel art adventure.
