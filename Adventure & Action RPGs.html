Adventure & Action RPGs

Epic worlds to explore and enemies to conquer

Immerse yourself in vast worlds filled with adventure, action, and storytelling. Adventure & Action RPGs offer rich narratives combined with exciting combat mechanics, allowing you to become the hero of your own epic journey.

God of War

Join Kratos and his son Atreus on an epic journey through Norse mythology. With its stunning visuals, intense combat, and emotional storytelling, God of War redefines the action-adventure genre.

God of War Ragnarok

The sequel to the acclaimed God of War (2018) continues the journey of Kratos and Atreus as they face the impending Ragnarok. Explore the nine realms and face new threats in this breathtaking adventure.

Elden Ring

From the creators of Dark Souls comes an epic open-world adventure. Explore the Lands Between, fight fearsome enemies, and uncover the mysteries of the Elden Ring in this challenging action RPG.

The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild

Embark on a journey across the vast land of Hyrule in this revolutionary open-world adventure. Climb mountains, glide through the skies, and solve puzzles in this masterpiece of game design.

The Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom

The sequel to Breath of the Wild takes Link to the skies above Hyrule. With new abilities like Ultrahand and Fuse, players can create unique solutions to puzzles and challenges.

Dragon Quest XI

A classic JRPG experience with modern visuals. Follow the Luminary on his quest to save the world of Erdrea from a powerful evil in this charming and engaging adventure.

The Legend of Zelda: Link's Awakening

A beautiful remake of the Game Boy classic. Help Link escape the mysterious Koholint Island in this charming adventure with a unique toy-like visual style.

Metroid Prime Remastered

The classic first-person adventure returns with enhanced visuals. Explore the alien world of Tallon IV as bounty hunter Samus Aran in this atmospheric sci-fi adventure.

Alan Wake 2

Dive into this psychological horror action-adventure that blends cinematic storytelling with intense gameplay. Follow dual protagonists in a mind-bending narrative about creative nightmares come to life.

Tunic

Play as a small fox on a big adventure in this isometric action game inspired by classic Zelda titles. Discover hidden paths, collect pages of an in-game manual, and uncover ancient secrets.
